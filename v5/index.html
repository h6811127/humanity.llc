We share a URL and an idea.

The idea is simple: your reputation should travel with you, not stay locked inside a single platform. Trust should be earned in public, not claimed in private. And the people using a system should help decide how it runs.

Now

Right now, there is no system. Just this page and an email address. We are manually verifying the first humans — one at a time, slowly, carefully.

Next

Public profiles. QR codes on stickers, bags, and hoodies. A simple trust score. A way for verified humans to find each other.

Eventually

Dispute resolution. Democratic governance. Public goods funded by the community. A charter that binds us all.
