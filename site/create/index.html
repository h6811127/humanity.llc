            Create a live card

            One tap registers your card on the network and issues a signed QR to print or share. Scans show live status - active, revoked, or expired - not a frozen link.

            Your owner key stays in this browser tab until you save it on the next screen.

            Card vs QR

            • Card Your live profile: handle, public line, status on the network. Not the same as profile ID alone.
            • QR Signed credential encoded in the code. It points at your card and can expire or be revoked separately from reprinting.
            • Profile ID Opaque network ID for your card (assigned at create). Printers and scanners use it; you rarely need to memorize it.
