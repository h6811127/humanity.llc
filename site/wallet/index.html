            How this works ›
            • Saved here means ownership stays in this browser until you clear site data. Not tied to an account or the network.
            • Open controls and Open card (⋯ menu) both load saved ownership when available, then open your card page. Open scan opens the public scan link only.
            • Default for vouching (⋯ on a saved card) auto-loads that card’s control when you open someone’s scan in this browser — you stay on the scan page to submit the attestation.
            • Network chip is live from the resolver (Card disabled vs QR revoked vs active). Card disabled since last visit compares to your last session — whole card off the network, not QR-only revoke.
            • Pin is a scan bookmark only (↗), no ownership.
