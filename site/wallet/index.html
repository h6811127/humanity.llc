How vouching works

  1. Use keys on a saved card (loads keys into this tab).
  2. Open someone else's scan link in this same browser.
  3. Scroll to Vouch on their scan page and submit your public statement.

You must be a Vouched Human (or steward) to vouch for others. Revoke and updates use the same active keys — open your card on /created/ after activating.
