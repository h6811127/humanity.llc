Menus + markets + surplus

A live menu becomes more useful when it can hand off to freshness tags and market passes instead of becoming one overloaded page.

Service now

  • Truck menu shows open, moved, or sold out
  • Market pass confirms the vendor is active today
  • Allergen notes stay visible at scan time

After service

  • Unsold stew becomes a freshness-tagged donation
  • Safe-to-take until 8 PM, then expires
  • Local resource card can point people to it

Pair with local economies, food freshness tags, and crisis resource cards.
