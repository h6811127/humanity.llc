What the scan page resolves first

  • Playable status Checkpoint found, route progress, local lore, and discovery rewards.
  • Maintenance status In service, last inspection, next due, and out-of-service warnings.
  • Authority boundary Safety warnings render above the game layer and cannot be overridden by points.

Who signs what

Game layer

  • Node discovered
  • Route progress
  • Unlock next checkpoint

Maintainer layer

  • In service / out of service
  • Inspection timestamp
  • Repair verification

Hard rule

  • No points for declaring equipment safe
  • No urgency theater to drive engagement
  • Maintenance copy outranks game copy

What the combined loop enables

Discovery

  • Players learn where the AED, filter, and trail nodes are
  • The scan teaches what good condition looks like
  • Curiosity increases public familiarity

Report

  • Player notices broken seal or missing cabinet key
  • Issue report enters maintainer review queue
  • Node pauses until someone signs the update

Repair

  • Maintainer verifies the fix
  • Safety state flips back to live
  • Local map unlocks the next route again

Pair with proof of maintenance and physical-world multiplayer.

The game can make people notice infrastructure; it must never become the authority that says the infrastructure is safe.
