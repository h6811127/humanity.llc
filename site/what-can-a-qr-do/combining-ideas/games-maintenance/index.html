Authority boundary on the page

The game is allowed to say

Node found, chapter unlocked, faction points awarded, next route visible.

The maintainer is allowed to say

In service, out of service, checked, repaired, do not use, reopened.

The page should never say

Players declared the AED safe, or game points overruled a maintenance warning.

Discovery

  • Players learn where neglected infrastructure actually lives
  • Trail markers and service nodes become visible parts of the map
  • Repair has a visible public consequence on next scan

Upkeep loop

  • Broken cover pauses the node
  • Maintainer signs fix
  • Game route unlocks again without rewriting the whole card

Failure to avoid

  • Gamifying false urgency around safety equipment
  • Letting player votes replace inspections
  • Hiding maintainer warnings below quest flavor text

Good combo: the game teaches people to notice boring infrastructure. Better combo: the scan still makes the maintainer the safety authority.
