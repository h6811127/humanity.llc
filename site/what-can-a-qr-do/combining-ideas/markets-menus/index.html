Rendering rules

Each vendor is a peer, not a subtenant of the market.

  • No platform fee on the scan Resolver returns signed state. Commerce stays at the vendor’s stall, on their terms
  • Freshness window is signed, not inferred “Safe to take” comes from a kitchen key with an explicit expiry, not from time-since-post
  • Vendor exits with their key A vendor that leaves the co-op keeps their menu key — portable to next operator, no lock-in

A printed pass becomes a shared surface for many peers — each speaking only for themselves, all visible in one scan.
