Combining ideas

The strong patterns stack: one QR stays a simple scan while the card behind it pulls from several signed, revocable streams — each with its own signer and limits.

Best combos keep authority clear: the game can reward discovery, but only the maintainer signs safety; the crisis card can list food help, but the kitchen signs freshness; the market pass can aggregate, but each vendor signs their own menu state.
