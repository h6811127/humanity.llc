Combining ideas

The strong patterns stack: one QR can stay a simple scan while the card behind it pulls from several signed, revocable streams.

These are demo scan pages, not shipped product behavior. The point is the shape: multiple streams, clear authority, no scan tracking by default.

One scan, several authorities

A card can show useful combined state without pretending one signer controls it all.

The best combos keep the scanner experience simple and the trust model honest. The game can reward discovery, but only the maintainer signs safety. The crisis card can show food, but the kitchen signs freshness.

Authority boundary

Good combo

  • Separate signed streams
  • Plain labels for who signed what
  • Revocation per card, object, batch, or vendor state

Bad combo

  • One page implies total authority
  • Game points blur safety status
