The pattern that makes stacking safe

Different authorities sign different claims. Authority separation is what makes multi-stream cards trustworthy, not just busy.

  • One QR, multiple signers The same sticker resolves to separate streams: game state from the organizer, safety status from the maintainer, food freshness from the kitchen
  • Authority is what makes combinations work The game can reward discovery, but only the maintainer signs safety. The crisis card can show food, but the kitchen signs freshness
  • Revocable per stream The kitchen can recall a food batch without killing the crisis card. The game organizer can pause a node without touching safety status

Why stacking works

Single stream

  • One signer controls everything
  • Any update touches the whole card
  • Mixed authority makes authority unclear
  • One revoke kills all state

Multiple streams

  • Each signer controls their own claim
  • Kitchen updates food · maintainer updates safety
  • Authority visible at scan time
  • Recall one batch · card keeps working

The scanner sees who signed what. That’s the trust mechanism.
