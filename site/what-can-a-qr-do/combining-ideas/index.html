The key rule

The game can reward discovery, but only the maintainer signs safety; the crisis card can show food, but the kitchen signs freshness.

Combining ideas works when the scan page behaves like a stacked digest of public truth, not one giant object that blurs who is accountable for each line.

  • Reward discovery Route operators sign play loops, checkpoints, and unlocks
  • Keep safety narrow Maintainers, kitchens, and organizers sign only the claims they own
  • Do not track the scanner The page can update resources without turning the person into data
