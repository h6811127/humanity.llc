Authority stays clear

Best combos keep authority visible: the game can reward discovery, but only the maintainer signs safety; the crisis card can show food, but the kitchen signs freshness.

Each stream has its own key. The scan page labels who signed what. Revoke one stream without killing the others. The QR stays simple — the network does the combining.

The pattern

Each combined page shows the same structural idea: multiple signed streams visible on one scan, with clear authority labels and independent revocability.

  • One QR, multiple streams The sticker encodes a single URL. The network returns status from every relevant signer.
  • Separate keys per authority Each signer controls only their claim. Game organizer ≠ maintainer ≠ kitchen ≠ co-op.
  • Independent revoke Kill one stream without affecting the others. Recall a food batch without revoking the market pass.
  • Labeled authority The scan page says who signed each section. No anonymous claims, no blended trust.
