  • Counterfeit cards detected · coalition revokes the series

  • Food-batch streams continue under host keys

Stale info is harm. Live signed streams shift the failure mode from “wrong number on a card” to “wrong row revoked.”

Boundaries by design

Functionality without extraction. No crisis CRM dressed as help.

  • No scan-trail by default Reading a hotline list never builds a dossier on the person reading it
  • Each kitchen owns its own freshness key Coalition cannot quietly mark a fridge batch “safe” on a host’s behalf
  • Save to device, not to platform “Save this resource” stores on the scanner’s phone — no “who saved what” feed

A card in someone’s pocket stays useful when the answer can change — and when no one had to become the product to read it.
