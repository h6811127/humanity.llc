What the scan page resolves first

  • Market status Which stalls are active, where they are, and whether the co-op pass is valid today.
  • Menu state Open or sold out, today’s items, allergen notes, and whether service has ended.
  • Surplus freshness Safe-to-take window, made time, expiry, allergens, and recall state for prepared food.

Who signs what

Organizer

  • Market active today
  • Approved stall and booth assignment
  • Co-op pass or member access state

Vendor

  • Menu items and sold-out state
  • Service hours and moved location
  • Current order or pickup copy

Kitchen

  • Safe-to-take surplus window
  • Made, expires, allergens
  • Recall or discard instructions

What the combined loop enables

During service

  • Market pass confirms the stall is legitimate today
  • Live menu answers what is sold out before someone waits in line
  • Member pricing stays tied to the pass, not the menu object

After service

  • Unsold prepared food becomes freshness-tagged surplus
  • Pickup window appears on the same market flow
  • Safe-to-take copy can expire automatically later that night

Keep claims clear

  • Organizer signs access and stall legitimacy
  • Vendor signs menu truth
  • Kitchen signs food safety

Pair with local economies, live menus, and food freshness tags.

The pass can coordinate discovery and pickup, but freshness still belongs to the kitchen and menu truth still belongs to the vendor.
