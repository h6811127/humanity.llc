Same idea elsewhere

One primitive: a physical thing points at current signed status, not a permanent file. QR works on any camera today. Tap and local mesh are next.

  • Poster or flyer Mutual-aid or event contact. Revoke when the run ends, not when stickers fade.
  • Sticker on merch Scan shows active or revoked; a stolen batch can be killed without reprinting the line
  • Member badge or card Club or co-op asks for live membership state, not a static plastic claim
