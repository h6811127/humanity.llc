FAQ

Questions we want you to ask

Isn't this just a QR profile?

A profile points at content. A Humanity Card is signed, revocable, vouched where shown, and designed for live control proof when needed. The QR is the doorway; the trust loop is the product.

Does buying the sticker make me verified?

No. Checkout funds the experiment and ships a pointer. Vouches come from real people under published rules — never from a cart.

What if someone steals my sticker?

A sticker is not identity proof. Revoke that item's QR credential; sibling credentials can stay active. Future scans show revoked status.

What does live control proof mean?

Recent evidence that the nearby person controls the card key — not legal ID, not human uniqueness, not because they bought something.

Is this a crypto or government ID project?

Neither. No token. No government ID collection in v1. Founder-built today, designed to become member-governed if the trust loop works.
