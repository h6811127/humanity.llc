Before you buy

Buying merch does not verify you. Vouches come from real people under public rules, never from checkout.

Why does checkout leave humanity.llc?

Shopify handles payments, tax, and order records. You complete checkout on their secure page, then return here for post-purchase guidance. We never store your card details.

What is a chromatic glitch unit?

On some Printify runs, DTF ink shifts part of the QR to blue or purple on fabric. We still see this on founding batches. These units are not guaranteed to scan reliably. They are honest variance in a limited run, not a separate SKU.

Are you only selling the Glitch hoodie right now?

Yes for checkout. The founding Glitch LIVE QR hoodie is the launch product. Personalized stickers and generic hoodies stay in preview on the customizer. The founding batch sticker is preview-only until we reopen Tier 0 checkout.
