FAQ

Does buying this verify me?

No. Verification and vouches come from the Humanity Card and human vouch rules - never from merch checkout.

What does a stranger see when they scan?

Live card status (active, revoked, suspended, or unknown), trust labels where applicable, plain limits, and CTAs to create a card or read the trust model - not proof that the wearer owns the card.

Can I revoke if I lose my phone?

Yes, if you saved an encrypted backup or recovery key at create. Import on /created/ from another device. See data policy for what we store.

What is Tier 1?

A later belonging drop for card holders - personalized artifacts when print QA allows. Same rule: buying never grants vouch status.
