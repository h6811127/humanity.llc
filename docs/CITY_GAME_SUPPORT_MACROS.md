# Cedar Rapids city game — support macros

## Revoked or compromised node

> That scan shows **public object state only**. If the sticker says “QR no longer valid,” the physical sign is unchanged — the live network marked that pointer off. There is **no scan log** of who visited. For a replacement QR, contact the season operator listed on the rules page.

---

## “I scanned but nothing unlocked for me personally”

> Season 1 is a **public goods** game — objects wake for the city, not a personal scoreboard. There is no rank, streak, or scan count on the page. Sharing seed clues helps the group complete the next chapter ([`/play/cedar-rapids/`](/play/cedar-rapids/)).

---

## Clue node / cabinet dilemma

> Some nodes present a **private-now vs shared-later** tradeoff in the story copy. You do not need an account to read public state. Deeper paths may require trust from a nearby witness node — that is place-linked legitimacy, not legal ID.

## Maintenance pause (node_14 / care stream)

> When the **care** line says paused or maintenance, treat that as site truth — **game bulletins are muted** until stewards clear the care stream. Players do not certify safety equipment.

## Temp drop expired (River Lantern)

## Privacy / tracking concern

> The reference operator does not store per-scan trails or heatmaps for gameplay. See [data policy](/data-policy.html) and the Cedar Rapids rules page. Collective unlocks use **voluntary site-code contribute** — a public count on the object, not hidden scan logs or operator quorum flips.

---

## “Is there a map of all the nodes?”

> The **city state board** on the rules page shows districts, unlock paths, and (when live) the same **public** relay/faction/fragment chips as scans — not your personal visits or location. There is no app map tracking where you walk. Scan any sticker for ground truth at that place. Engineering plan: [`CITY_GAME_MAP_DASHBOARD.md`](CITY_GAME_MAP_DASHBOARD.md) (internal).

## Wrong or offensive bulletin

> Bulletins are operator-published world state. Report to `[season operator contact]` with node label + photo. Emergency safety issues go to local authorities — game nodes are not safety certification.
